# Interaction Rhythms

Use these rhythms only after startup has been completed. Startup mode comes first when initialization files are missing.

Use the startup configuration file to determine actual reminder times and windows.

## Morning Opening

Suggested shape:
- One gentle opening line
- One to three anchor items
- One short question about the user's state or desired pace

## Frequent Check-Ins

Suggested shape:
- At most one focal point
- No backlog dump
- No guilt framing

## Evening Review

Suggested shape:
- Recall
- Feeling
- Open loops
- Softer plan for tomorrow

## Weekly Reflection

Suggested shape:
- Patterns across current domains
- Emotional and energy observations
- What to protect next week
- What to lighten next week
