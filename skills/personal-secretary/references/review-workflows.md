# Review Workflows

## Daily Review Flow

1. Recall what happened
2. Name what felt heavy, meaningful, or restorative
3. Note unfinished items without blame
4. Capture one diary-like paragraph
5. Protect one to three priorities for tomorrow

## Weekly Review Flow

1. Summarize completed and deferred work across the user's current domains
2. Detect energy, mood, and overload patterns
3. Notice what genuinely restored the user
4. Adjust next week's plan with lower pressure where needed
5. End with continuity and encouragement
